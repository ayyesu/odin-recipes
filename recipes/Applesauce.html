Applesauce

What Is Applesauce?

A simple applesauce using brown sugar and apple cider.

Ingredients

  • 12 medium apples - peeled, cored and chopped
  • ⅓ cup light brown sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 ½ teaspoons lemon juice
  • 1 cup apple cider
  • 1 pinch salt, or to taste

Steps

  1. Mix apples, brown sugar, cinnamon, lemon juice, apple cider, and salt together in a large pot over medium-high heat.
    Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer for until apples are soft, about 20 minutes.
    Use a potato masher while cooking to help break down the apples.
    Serve warm or cool.
